Our Story

Healing with Heart & Science

Founded in 2010, MediCare Plus was built on a simple belief: every patient deserves world-class care delivered with compassion. From a small clinic, we have grown into a multi-specialty healthcare centre serving thousands of patients.

Our team of 50+ specialist doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals work together to provide integrated care using the latest medical technology and evidence-based practices.
